# Research Documentation

This directory contains the formal research documents produced during the early stages of the Master’s thesis in Data Analytics.

The materials reflect the academic progression of the research, from initial topic definition to a detailed research proposal.

---

## Documents Included

### 1. Basic Research Proposal (Power Point document)

This presentation outlines the initial definition of the research topic and includes:
- The proposed research title and topic area
- Background and motivation for the study
- Initial research objectives
- Identification of the domain area and problem area

This document outlines the initial research idea and defines the scope of the study.

---

### 2. Research Proposal (Written Document)

This document expands on the initial proposal and includes:
- Research title
- Defined research objectives
- A literature review
- Proposed research methodology
- Ethical considerations

The proposal serves as the foundation for the experimental work and implementation stages of the thesis.

---

## Notes

- All documents are produced strictly for academic purposes.
- The research focuses on evaluating and comparing analytics pipelines and data engineering frameworks/tools.
